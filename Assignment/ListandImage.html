Ingrediants

  • Basmati rice
  • Mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potato)
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Curd
  • Biryani masala
  • Oil or ghee

Biryani Preparation

  1. Wash the basmati rice and soak it for 20 minutes.
  2. Boil the rice with bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, ghee, and salt until 90% cooked.
  3. Heat oil and ghee in a pan and add cumin seeds, bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, and cinnamon.
  4. Add sliced onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chillies and cook for a minute.
  6. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  7. Add vegetables like carrots, beans, peas, potatoes, and cauliflower.
  8. Mix in biryani masala, turmeric, red chilli powder, coriander powder, garam masala, curd, mint, and coriander leaves.
  9. Cook the vegetables until they become soft and the masala thickens.
  10. In a heavy-bottom pot, spread a layer of vegetable masala.
  11. Add a layer of the partially cooked rice over it.
  12. Sprinkle mint, coriander, fried onions, or cashews (optional).
  13. Repeat the layering until everything is used.
  14. Cover the pot tightly and cook on low flame for 10–15 minutes (dum).
  15. Gently fluff the biryani and serve hot.
